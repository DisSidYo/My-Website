If you want to write a book on me, look no further!

Till the year 2020, I had things pretty easy - good school, amazing friends, and heap of opportunities. When things are served to you on a platter, sometimes you take them for granted. That is exactly what happened with me. In my 11th Grade, Covid-19 struck and life became miserable. I was preparing for JEE (Joint Entrance Examination) which is the entrance exam to get into Indian Engineering Colleges. I did not do well in it, but was fortunate enough to secure a place at UTS.

My learnings

  1. Life gives you joys and sorrows. Both are valuable lessons.

  2. If you keep quiet in Class (especially if you are studying online), you are in deep trouble. Always try to be interactive in classes.

  3. Respect the people who are struggling in their lives to make your life easy.

  4. Be open to ideas, opportunities, and people.

  5. Be Happy, and make others Happy.

Siddharth Wani, University of Technology Sydney, WebAss Spring 2022
