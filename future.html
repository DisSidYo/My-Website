Being through so much in this pandemic, I reckon everyone wants some sort respite from the feeling of loneliness. I want to make lots of friends and connections, expand my network, travel and explore places and obviously make the most out of my Uni-Experience. I want to gain practical work experience in my undergrad as well, as it is vital for anybody looking to work in IT.

Most importantly, I want to decide my major. UTS provides an opportunity to select majors from a pool of options, so it is very important for me to choose the one I like. Doing something that genuinely gives me pleasure and satisfaction is my ultimate goal.

Also, being an international student, finances are obviously a bit burdening. I want to get as much scholarship as possible, and also some kind of a part-time job to lessen the load on my parents.

I want to ensure that my Uni-Days are the most colorful days of my life. I want to learn, I want to grow, I want to achieve, and last but not the least, I want to make my family proud.

Siddharth Wani, University of Technology Sydney, WebAss Spring 2022
